Create Salesforce Opportunity Trigger

Implement an Apex trigger for the Opportunity object that automatically:
- Updates the opportunity owner's manager when opportunity amount exceeds $100,000
- Creates follow-up tasks for high-value opportunities
- Prevents deletion of opportunities with closed won status
- Logs all changes to a custom audit object

Priority: Critical
Story Points: 5
Assignee: Salesforce Developer
Labels: Apex, Trigger, Automation
